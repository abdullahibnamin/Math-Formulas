# Math Formulas

### 1. Differentiation Formulas

1. i) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{sin\theta}{\theta} = 1 $  
   
   ii) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{tan\theta}{\theta} = 1 $  
 
2. i) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\theta}{sin\theta} = 1 $  
   
   ii) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\theta}{tan\theta} = 1 $  

3. i) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}x^{n} = nx^{n-1}$  
  
   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\frac{1}{x^{n}} = \frac{-n}{x^{n+1}}$  

   iii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\sqrt{x} = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}$   
   
   
4. i) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}e^{x} = e^{x}$  
 
   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}a^{x} = a^{x} \ln \cdot a$  

5. i) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\ln x = \frac{1}{x}$  

   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\log_a{x} = \frac{1}{x}\log_a{e}$  

6. i) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}sinx = cosx$  

   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}cosx = - sinx$  

   iii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}tanx = sec^{2}x$  
   
   iv) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}cotx = - cosec^{2}x$  
   
   v) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}secx = secx \cdot tanx$  
   
   vi) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}cosecx = - cosecx \cdot cotx$  


7. i) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}sin^{-1}x = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}$  
   
   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}cos^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}$  
   
   iii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}tan^{-1}x = \frac{1}{1+x^{2}}$  

   iv) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}cot^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{1+x^{2}}$  

   v) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}sec^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{x \sqrt{x^{2} - 1}}$  
   
   vi) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}cosec^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{x \sqrt{x^{2} - 1}}$  

8. $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}(u-v+w) = \frac{d}{dx} u - \frac{d}{dx} v + \frac{d}{dx} w$  

9. $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}(u \cdot v) = u \cdot \frac{d}{dx}(v) + v \cdot \frac{d}{dx} (u) $  

10. $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\frac{u}{v} = \frac{v \cdot \frac{d}{dx}(u) - u \cdot \frac{d}{dx} (v)}{v^{2}} $  

11. $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx} e^{x} \cdot u = e^{x}(u+\frac{d}{dx} \cdot u)$

12. i) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx} u^{v} = u^{v} \cdot \frac{d}{dx}(v \cdot \ln u)$  
    
    ii) $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx} x^{x} = x^{x} (\ln x + 1)$  
     

### 2. Integration Formulas
13. i) $\int x^{n} dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}$  
    ii) $\int \frac{1}{x^{n}} dx = \frac{-1}{(n-1)x^{n-1}}$  

14. i) $\int e^{x} dx = e^{x}$  
    ii) $\int a^{x} dx = \frac {a^{x}}{\ln a}$  

15. i) $\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \ln x$  
    ii) $\int \ln x dx = x \cdot \ln x - x$  

16. i) $\int sinx dx = - cosx$  
    ii) $\int cosx dx = sinx$  

17. i) $\int tanx dx = - \ln cosx = \ln secx$  
    ii) $\int cotx dx = \ln sinx = - \ln cosecx$  

18. i) $\int secx dx = \ln tan (\frac{\pi}{4} + \frac{x}{2})$  
    ii) $\int cosecx dx = \ln tan \frac{x}{2}$  

19. i) $\int sec^{2}x dx = tanx$  
    ii) $\int cosec^{2}x dx = - cotx $  

20. i) $\int secx \cdot tanx dx = secx$  
    ii) $\int cosecx \cdot cotx dx = - cosecx$  

21. i) $\int \frac{dx}{a^{2} + x^{2}} = \frac{1}{a} tan^{-1}\frac{x}{a}$  
    ii) $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{a^{2} - x^{2}}} = sin^{-1}\frac{x}{a}$  

22. i) $\int \frac{dx}{a^{2} - x^{2}} = \frac{1}{2a} \ln \frac{a+x}{a-x}$  
    ii) $\int \frac{dx}{x^{2} - a^{2}} = \frac{1}{2a} \ln \frac{x-a}{x+a}$ 

23. $\int (u \cdot v) dx = u \cdot \int v \cdot dx - \int (\frac{d}{dx}u \cdot \int v \cdot dx) dx $

### 4. Fundamental Relation
1. $sin^{2}A + cos^{2}A = 1$  
2. $sec^{2}A - tan^{2}A = 1$  
3. $cosec^{2}A - cotA = 1$


### 5. Fundamental Theorem
4. $sin(A+B) = sinA \cdot cosB + cosA \cdot sinB$  
5. $sin(A-B) = sinA \cdot cosB - cosA \cdot sinB$  
6. $cos(A+B) = cosA \cdot cosB - sinA \cdot sinB$  
7. $cos(A-B) = cosA \cdot cosB + sinA \cdot sinB$


### 6. Division Method
8. $tan(A+B) = \frac{tanA + tanB} {1 - tanA \cdot tanB}$  

9. $tan(A-B) = \frac{tanA - tanB} {1 + tanA \cdot tanB}$  

10. $cot(A+B) = \frac{cotA \cdot cotB - 1} {cotB + cotA}$  

11. $cot(A-B) = \frac{cotA \cdot cotB + 1} {cotB - cotA}$  


### 7. Multiplication Method
$$
\begin{align*}
sin(A+B) \cdot sin(A-B) = sin^{2}A - sin^{2}B \\
= cos^{2}B - cos^{2}A
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
cos(A+B) \cdot cos(A-B) = cos^{2}A - sin^{2}B \\
= cos^{2}B - sin^{2}A
\end{align*}
$$

14. $tan(A+B) \cdot tan(A-B) = \frac{tan^{2}A-tan^{2}B}{1-tan^{2}A \cdot tan^{2}B}$

15. $cot(A+B) \cdot cot(A-B) = \frac{1- cot^{2}A \cdot cot^{2}B}{cot^{2}A - cot^{2}B}$


### 8. Addition & Subtraction
16. $sin(A+B) + sin(A-B) = 2sinA \cdot cosB$  
17. $sin(A+B) - sin(A-B) = 2cosA \cdot sinB$  
18. $cos(A+B) + cos(A-B) = 2cosA \cdot cosB$  
19. $cos(A-B) - cos(A+B) = 2sinA \cdot sinB$


### 9. Transformation Method
20. $sinC + sinD = 2 \cdot sin\frac{C+D}{2} \cdot cos\frac{C-D}{2}$ 

21. $sinC - sinD = 2 \cdot cos\frac{C+D}{2} \cdot sin\frac{C-D}{2}$  

22. $cosC + cosD = 2 \cdot cos\frac{C+D}{2} \cdot cos\frac{C-D}{2}$  

23. $cosC - cosD = 2 \cdot sin\frac{C+D}{2} \cdot sin\frac{D-C}{2}$



### 10. Double Angle Formula
24. i) $sin2A = 2 \cdot sinA \cdot cosA $  
    ii) $sin2A = \frac{2tanA}{1 + tan^{2}A}$  

25. i) $cos2A = cos^{2}A - sin^{2}A$  
    ii) $cos2A = 2cos^{2}A - 1$  
    iii) $cos2A = 1- 2sin^{2}A$  
    iv) $cos2A = \frac{1 - tan^{2}A}{1 + tan^{2}A}$  

26. i) $tan2A = \frac{2tanA}{1 - tan^{2}A}$  
    ii) $cot2A = \frac{cot^{2}A - 1}{2cotA}$

27. i) $sin^{2}A = \frac{1}{2}(1-cos2A)$  
    ii) $cos^{2}A = \frac{1}{2}(1+cos2A)$  
    iii) $sinA \cdot cosA = \frac{1}{2}(sin2A)$


### 11. Triple Angle Formula
28. i) $sin3A = 3sinA - 4sin^{3}A$  
    ii) $cos3A = 4cos^{3}A - 3cosA$  
    iii) $tan3A = \frac{3tanA - tan^{3}A}{1-3tan^{2}A}$  
    iv) $cot3A = \frac{3cotA - cot^{3}A}{1-3cot^{2}A}$  

29. i) $sin^{3}A = \frac{1}{4}(3sinA - sin3A)$  
    ii) $cos^{3}A = \frac{1}{4}(3cosA + cos3A)$  
    iii) $tan^{3}A = 3tanA + tan3A(3tan^{2}A - 1)$  
    iv) $cot^{3}A = 3cotA + cot3A(3cot^{2}A - 1)$  

### 12. Equation
30. i) $sin\theta = sin\alpha$ then $\theta = n\pi+(-1)^{n} \cdot \alpha $  
    ii) $cos\theta = cos\alpha$ then $\theta = 2n\pi \pm \alpha $  
    iii) $tan\theta = tan\alpha$ then $\theta = n\pi + \alpha $  


31. i) $sin\theta = 0$ then $\theta = n\pi $  
    ii) $cos\theta = 0$ then $\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}(2n+1)$  


32. i) $sin\theta = \pm 1$ then $\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}(4n \pm 1)$  
    ii) $cos\theta = 1$ then $\theta = 2n \pi$  
    iii) $cos\theta = -1$ then $\theta = \pi(2n+1)$  
    iv) $tan\theta = \pm 1$ then $\theta = n\pi \pm \frac{\pi}{4} $  


### 13. Inverse Function
33. i) $tan^{-1}x \pm tan^{-1}y = tan^{-1}\frac{x \pm y}{1 \mp xy}$  
    
    ii) $sin^{-1}x \pm sin^{-1}y = sin^{-1}(x\sqrt{1-y^{2}} \pm y\sqrt{1-x^{2}})$  
    
    iii) $cos^{-1}x \pm cos^{-1}y = cos^{-1} \lbrace xy \mp \sqrt{(1-x^{2})(1-y^{2})} \rbrace$  


34. i) $sin^{-1}\frac{2x}{1+x^{2}} = 2tan^{-1}x$  
    
    ii) $cos^{-1}\frac{1-x^{2}}{1+x^{2}} = 2tan^{-1}x$  
    
    iii) $tan^{-1}\frac{2x}{1-x^{2}} = 2tan^{-1}x$


### 14. Properties of Triangles
35. $\frac{a}{sinA} = \frac{b}{sinB} = \frac{c}{sinC} = 2R$  

36. $cosA = \frac{b^{2}+c^{2}-a^{2}}{2bc}$

37. i) $a = b \cdot cosC + c \cdot cosB$  
    
    ii) $b = c \cdot cosA + a \cdot cosC$  
    
    iii) $c = a \cdot cosB + b \cdot cosA$
