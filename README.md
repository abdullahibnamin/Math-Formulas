# Math Formulas

### 1. Differentiation Formulas

1. i) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{sin\theta}{\theta} = 1 $  
   
   ii) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{tan\theta}{\theta} = 1 $  
 
2. i) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\theta}{sin\theta} = 1 $  
   
   ii) $\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\theta}{tan\theta} = 1 $  

3. i) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}x^{n} = nx^{n-1}$  
  
   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\frac{1}{x^{n}} = \frac{-n}{x^{n+1}}$  

   iii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\sqrt{x} = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}$   
   
   
4. i) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}e^{x} = e^{x}$  
 
   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}a^{x} = a^{x} \ln \cdot a$  

5. i) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\ln x = \frac{1}{x}$  

   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\log_a{x} = \frac{1}{x}\log_a{e}$  

6. i) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}sinx = cosx$  

   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}cosx = - sinx$  

   iii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}tanx = sec^{2}x$  
   
   iv) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}cotx = - cosec^{2}x$  
   
   v) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}secx = secx \cdot tanx$  
   
   vi) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}cosecx = - cosecx \cdot cotx$  


7. i) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}sin^{-1}x = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}$  
   
   ii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}cos^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}$  
   
   iii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}tan^{-1}x = \frac{1}{1+x^{2}}$  

   iv) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}cot^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{1+x^{2}}$  

   v) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}sec^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{x \sqrt{x^{2} - 1}}$  
   
   vi) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}cosec^{-1}x = \frac{- 1}{x \sqrt{x^{2} - 1}}$  

8. $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(u-v+w) = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} u - \frac{\partial}{\partial x} v + \frac{\partial}{\partial x} w$  

9. $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(u \cdot v) = u \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(v) + v \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x} (u) $  

10. $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\frac{u}{v} = \frac{v \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(u) - u \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x} (v)}{v^{2}} $  

11. $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x} e^{x} \cdot u = e^{x}(u+\frac{\partial}{\partial x} \cdot u)$

12. i) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x} u^{v} = u^{v} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(v \cdot \ln u)$  
    
    ii) $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial x} x^{x} = x^{x} (\ln x + 1)$

### 4. Fundamental Relation
1. $sin^{2}A + cos^{2}A = 1$  
2. $sec^{2}A - tan^{2}A = 1$  
3. $cosec^{2}A - cotA = 1$


### 5. Fundamental Theorem
4. sin(A+B) = sinAcosB + cosAsinB  
5. sin(A-B) = sinAcosB - cosAsinB  
6. cos(A+B) = cosAcosB - sinAsinB  
7. cos(A-B) = cosAcosB + sinAsinB


### 6. Division Method
8. $tan(A+B) = \frac{tanA + tanB} {1 - tanA \cdot tanB}$  

9. $tan(A-B) = \frac{tanA - tanB} {1 + tanA \cdot tanB}$  

10. $cot(A+B) = \frac{cot \cdot AcotB - 1} {cotB + cotA}$  

11. $cot(A-B) = \frac{cotA \cdot cotB + 1} {cotB - cotA}$  


### 7. Multiplication Method
$$
\begin{align*}
sin(A+B)sin(A-B) = sin^{2}A - sin^{2}B \\
= cos^{2}B - cos^{2}A
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
cos(A+B)cos(A-B) = cos^{2}A - sin^{2}B \\
= cos^{2}B - sin^{2}A
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
tan(A+B)tan(A-B) = \frac{tan^{2}A-tan^{2}B}{1-tan^{2}Atan^{2}B}
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
cot(A+B)cot(A-B) = \frac{1- cot^{2}Acot^{2}B}{cot^{2}A - cot^{2}B}
\end{align*}
$$

### 10. Double Angle Formula
24. i) $sin2A = 2 \cdot sinA \cdot cosA $  
    ii) $sin2A = \frac{2tanA}{1 + tan^{2}A}$  

25. i) $cos2A = cos^{2}A - sin^{2}A$  
    ii) $cos2A = 2cos^{2} - 1$  
    iii) $cos2A = 1- 2sin^{2}$  
    iv) $cos2A = \frac{1 - tan^{2}A}{1 + tan^{2}A}$  

26. i) $tan2A = \frac{2tanA}{1 - tan^{2}A}$  
    ii) $cot2A = \frac{cot^{2}A - 1}{2cotA}$

27. i) $sin^{2}A = \frac{1}{2}(1-cos2A)$  
    ii) $cos^{2}A = \frac{1}{2}(1+cos2A)$  
    iii) $sinA \cdot cosA = \frac{1}{2}(sin2A)$


### 11. Triple Angle Formula
28. i) $sin3A = 3sinA - 4sin^{3}A$  
    ii) $cos3A = 4cos^{3}A - 3cosA$  
    iii) $tan3A = \frac{3tanA - tan^{3}A}{1-3tan^{2}A}$  
    iv) $cot3A = \frac{3cotA - cot^{3}A}{1-3cot^{2}A}$  

29. i) $sin^{3}A = \frac{1}{4}(3sinA - sin3A)$  
    ii) $cos^{3}A = \frac{1}{4}(3cosA + cos3A)$  
    iii) $tan^{3}A = 3tanA + tan3A(3tan^{2}A - 1)$  
    iv) $cot^{3}A = 3cotA + cot3A(3cot^{2}A - 1)$  

### 12. Equation
30. i) $sin\theta = sin\alpha$ then $\theta = n\pi+(-1)^{n} \alpha $  
    ii) $cos\theta = cos\alpha$ then $\theta = 2n\pi \pm \alpha $  
    iii) $tan\theta = tan\alpha$ then $\theta = n\pi + \alpha $  


31. i) $sin\theta = 0$ then $\theta = n\pi $  
    ii) $cos\theta = 0$ then $\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}(2n+1)$  


32. i) $sin\theta = \pm 1$ then $\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}(4n \pm 1)$  
    ii) $cos\theta = 1$ then $\theta = 2n \pi$  
    iii) $cos\theta = -1$ then $\theta = \pi(2n+1)$  
    iv) $tan\theta = \pm 1$ then $\theta = n\pi \pm \frac{\pi}{4} $  


### 13. Inverse Function
33. i) $tan^{-1}x \pm tan^{-1}y = tan^{-1}\frac{x \pm y}{1 \mp xy}$  
    
    ii) $sin^{-1}x \pm sin^{-1}y = sin^{-1}(x\sqrt{1-y^{2}} \pm y\sqrt{1-x^{2}})$  
    
    iii) $cos^{-1}x \pm cos^{-1}y = cos^{-1} \{xy \mp \sqrt{(1-x^{2})(1-y^{2})} \}$  


34. i) $sin^{-1}\frac{2x}{1+x^{2}} = 2tan^{-1}x$  
    
    ii) $cos^{-1}\frac{1-x^{2}}{1+x^{2}} = 2tan^{-1}x$  
    
    iii) $tan^{-1}\frac{2x}{1-x^{2}} = 2tan^{-1}x$
